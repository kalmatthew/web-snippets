reference: Player's Handbook, page 143

Standard coin conversion rates:

| Coin | CP   | SP   | EP   | GP    | PP     |
| ---- | ---- | ---- | ---- | ----- | ------ |
| CP   | 1    | 1/10 | 1/50 | 1/100 | 1/1000 |
| SP   | 10   | 1    | 1/5  | 1/10  | 1/100  |
| EP   | 50   | 5    | 1    | 1/2   | 1/20   |
| GP   | 100  | 10   | 2    | 1     | 1/10   |
| PP   | 1000 | 100  | 50   | 10    | 1      |

| Abbreviation | Name     |
| ------------ | -------- |
| CP           | Copper   |
| SP           | Silver   |
| EP           | Electrum |
| GP           | Gold     |
| PP           | Platinum |
